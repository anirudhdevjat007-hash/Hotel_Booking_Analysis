# 🏨 Hotel Booking Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to identify booking trends, cancellation patterns, customer behavior, and business performance using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

The objective is to help hotel management make data-driven decisions to reduce cancellations and improve revenue.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze hotel booking trends
- Identify factors affecting cancellations
- Compare City Hotel and Resort Hotel performance
- Analyze customer segments
- Study lead time and ADR
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard

## 💡 Key Insights

- City Hotel has more bookings than Resort Hotel.
- Longer lead times are associated with higher cancellation rates.
- Non-refundable deposits have the highest cancellation percentage.
- Online Travel Agents contribute the largest share of bookings.
- Customers with more special requests are less likely to cancel.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations

- Offer incentives for early confirmation.
- Reduce dependency on high-cancellation booking channels.
- Encourage refundable booking policies where appropriate.
- Improve customer engagement for high lead-time bookings.
